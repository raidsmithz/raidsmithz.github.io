Smart Chicken Coop with Automatic Feeder, Waste Cleaner, and Alert System

Project information

  • Category: Embedded, IoT
  • Completion: July 2023 - October 2023

This project focuses on the creation of a cutting-edge chicken coop equipped with advanced features to streamline poultry rearing processes. The smart coop incorporates an automatic feeder and waste cleaner, providing convenience for poultry farmers by automating feeding and waste removal tasks. The automatic feeder ensures that chickens receive timely and precise feeding, promoting their health and productivity. Meanwhile, the waste cleaner efficiently removes waste from the coop, maintaining cleanliness and hygiene in the poultry environment. In addition to its automation capabilities, the smart chicken coop features an alert system that provides real-time notifications for maintenance needs. This alert system helps poultry farmers stay informed about any issues or malfunctions in the coop, allowing them to take prompt action to address them and ensure the continued well-being of their chickens. Overall, this innovative project aims to enhance efficiency, productivity, and welfare in poultry farming through the implementation of smart technologies and automation.
