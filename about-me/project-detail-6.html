Automated Perforated Metal Filter Cleaner with Adjustable Air Pressure Control

Project information

  • Category: Embedded
  • Completion: July 2023 - September 2023

This project focuses on the development of an automated system designed to streamline maintenance tasks for perforated metal filters. The system incorporates an automatic cleaner equipped with adjustable air pressure and filter rotation speed, providing efficient and customizable cleaning capabilities. Utilizing Arduino microcontrollers alongside relays and potentiometers, the system enables precise control over air pressure levels and filter rotation speeds, ensuring optimal cleaning performance. Additionally, an LCD 16x2 display is integrated for real-time monitoring and control of the cleaning process, enhancing user convenience and efficiency. By automating the cleaning process and providing adjustable parameters, this project aims to improve the effectiveness and ease of maintenance for perforated metal filters in various applications.
