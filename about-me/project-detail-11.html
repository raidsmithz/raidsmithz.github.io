Aquarium Monitoring System with pH and Temperature Control

Project information

  • Category: Embedded, IoT
  • Completion: September 2023 - November 2023

This project focuses on the creation of a sophisticated aquarium monitoring system designed to ensure optimal water conditions for aquatic species. The system incorporates pH and temperature control mechanisms, allowing users to maintain precise water parameters tailored to the specific needs of their fish and other aquatic inhabitants. By continuously monitoring and adjusting pH levels and temperature, the system promotes a healthy and thriving aquatic environment. Additionally, the aquarium monitoring system offers IoT control via Blynk, enabling users to remotely monitor and manage their aquariums from anywhere with an internet connection. Through the Blynk app, users can access real-time data on pH levels and temperature, receive alerts for any deviations from set parameters, and adjust control settings as needed. This remote monitoring and management capability enhances convenience and peace of mind for aquarium owners, ensuring that their aquatic ecosystems remain stable and healthy even when they are away from home.
