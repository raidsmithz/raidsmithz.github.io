ICITACEE 2022 Wordpress Website Management

Project information

  • Category: Web
  • Completion: Jan 2022 - May 2022

The ICITACEE 2022 website served as a dynamic platform for branding and facilitating communication among participants of "The 9th International Conference on Information Technology, Computer, and Electrical Engineering." By providing a user-friendly interface and comprehensive information, the website played a vital role in fostering discussion, dissemination, and networking among researchers, academics, professionals, and students engaged in various technology-related disciplines. This platform served as a forum for sharing knowledge, exploring innovative ideas, and addressing global challenges in information technology, computer systems, electrical engineering, and interdisciplinary studies. Through effective project management and technical skills, I ensured that the website contributed to the success of ICITACEE 2022 by providing a seamless and engaging experience for all participants.
