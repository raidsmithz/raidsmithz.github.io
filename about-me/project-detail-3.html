FPGA Programming

Project information

  • Category: Embedded
  • Completion: Feb 2023 - Jun 2023

The laboratory utilizes the Spartan-3E module as the FPGA microprocessor, supplemented with components such as 8-segment LEDs, buttons, and standard LEDs. With these resources, students are guided in implementing FPGA designs, which range from simple projects like traffic lights and calculators to more complex ones such as counters and countdown timers. Through these hands-on design projects, students gain practical experience in FPGA application development, honing their skills and deepening their understanding of digital design concepts. As a Teaching Assistant, my goal is to support and empower students in their learning journey, preparing them for future challenges and opportunities in the field of FPGA technology.
