Development of Smart Car Detection System with Speed and Positioning Monitoring

Project information

  • Category: Embedded
  • Completion: Jan 2024 - Mar 2024

This project focuses on the creation of a smart car detection system capable of monitoring vehicle speed and positioning. The system utilizes Arduino microcontrollers for vehicle detection based on inter-vehicle distance and speed, employing buzzer alerts for real-time notifications. Additionally, the project incorporates Lidar TF03 sensors for precise measurement of inter-vehicle distances, ensuring accurate detection and safety management. Gravity: GNSS GPS BeiDou technology is implemented to track vehicle speed, providing comprehensive monitoring capabilities. The project aims to enhance road safety and traffic management by enabling efficient detection and monitoring of vehicle movements, facilitating timely intervention and response to potential hazards.
