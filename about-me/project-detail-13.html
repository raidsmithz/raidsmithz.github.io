Development of an Arduino-Based Smart Oven with PID Heater Control for Automated Temperature and Time Regulation

Project information

  • Category: Embedded
  • Completion: September 2023

This project focuses on the creation of a smart oven system integrated with Arduino microcontrollers and PID (Proportional-Integral-Derivative) control algorithms to automate temperature regulation and time management during cooking processes. The system enables users to set desired cooking parameters, including temperature and duration, via user-friendly interfaces. The Arduino microcontroller, equipped with PID control, continuously monitors and adjusts the heating element to maintain precise temperature levels, ensuring optimal cooking results. Additionally, the system incorporates timers to manage cooking durations, providing convenience and flexibility to users. By leveraging the capabilities of Arduino and PID control, this project aims to enhance cooking efficiency, consistency, and convenience, offering a seamless and automated cooking experience for modern households.
