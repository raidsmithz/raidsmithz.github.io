Surveillance Camera Based on Deep Learning and Internet of Things as a Step Towards Facing the New Normal Post-Covid-19 Era

Project information

  • Category: Embedded, IoT
  • Completion: June 2021 - October 2021

The project titled "Surveillance Camera Based on Deep Learning and Internet of Things as a Step Towards Facing the New Normal Post-Covid-19 Era" involves the development of a device capable of detecting individuals and measuring distances between them. This device provides real-time indicators of social distancing violations through an integrated buzzer system. Additionally, it features an Internet of Things (IoT) system for remote monitoring by authorities. Key components of the project include training models for deep learning-based person detection, creating programs for person detection, distance calculation, and image perspective calculation, as well as developing an Android monitoring application for remote IoT system monitoring.
